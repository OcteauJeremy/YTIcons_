About YTIcons

Buy, collect and sell your favorite YouTuber unique cards (YTIcons) using blockchain technology ! Once you purchase a YTIcon, an algorithm will increase the price and your YTIcon will directly be handed to the marketplace. But do not worry, if someone buys it back, you will get paid in receiving more than the amount you originally invested in ETH !

How does it work ?

YTIcons uses the same blockchain technology as Ethereum which features the smart contract functionality : each and every YTIcon is linked to an unique token on the blockchain. To purchase a YTIcon, you can simply send Ether to the contract using MetaMask. You can download the latter's extension on most of the browsers (e.g. Chrome or Firefox).
