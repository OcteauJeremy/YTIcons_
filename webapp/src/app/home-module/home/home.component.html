About YTIcons

Buy, collect and sell your favorite YouTuber evolutive and unique cards (YTIcons) using blockchain technology ! Once you purchase a YTIcon, an algorithm will increase the price and your YTIcon will directly be handed to the marketplace. But do not worry, if someone buys it back, you will get paid by receiving more than the amount you originally invested in ETH !

The price increase of the YTIcons' is done this way : x2 from 0 to 0.05 ETH, x1.5 from 0.05 to 0.5 ETH, x1.25 from 0.5 to 1 ETH and x1.15 from 1 ETH to infinity.

How does it work ?

YTIcons uses the same blockchain technology as Ethereum which features the smart contract functionality : each and every YTIcon is linked to an unique token on the blockchain.

Moreover, each of the YTIcons' statistics are continuously updated and once it reaches enough followers, the concerned YTIcon will evolve and get another design !

To purchase a YTIcon, you can simply visit the marketplace and send Ether to the contract using MetaMask.
