Operation

Because we are really concerned about the relationship between YTIcons and its community, we want to provide a total transparency for all of the current and future YTIcollectors.

YTIcons is a unique Smart Contract (also referred as “SC”) and every YouTuber card is one Smart Contract token, i.e. once you purchase a card, it belongs to you and nobody else can steal it from you except if one person buys it from you.

At every purchase, the bought YouTuber card (referred as “YTIcon”) will get its price increased. This rise is carefully calculated by the SC to avoid any excess and to allow you to gain up to :

  • x2 from 0 to 0.05 ETH.
  • x1.5 from 0.05 to 0.5 ETH.
  • x1.25 from 0.5 to 1 ETH.
  • x1.15 from 1 ETH to infinity.

Scenario

Let’s consider that you purchase a YTIcon for 0.1 ETH and get your transaction validated by the blockchain : it is yours and its price increases (x1.5 since 0.1 ETH is between 0.05 and 0.5 ETH) so its value is now around* 0.15 ETH.

You can set the price from the previous YTIcon's price (0.1 ETH) up to its current price (0.15 ETH), but you are convinced that the YTIcon you bought is worth its value.

You were right since someone else directly buys it from you and get his/her transaction validated by the blockchain. For this transaction (note that we will use the example values but the percentages cited below are inscribed in the Smart Contract and cannot be modified in any way) :

  • The seller gets 92% of the price. In this case, as your YTIcon is worth 0.15 ETH, you will gain around 0.14 ETH.
  • If the YTIcon is verified by a YouTuber, 3% will be sent to an address she/he carefully chose. On the other hand, if the YTIcon is not verified, these 3% will be sent to an address especially created for charity purposes.
  • With the remaining percentages, 1% will be sent to an utility fund created for YTIcons’ development which includes giveaway, events’ rewards and more.
  • Finally 4% will be shared between all of the YTIcons’ creators and will be used for YTIcons’ development including advertisements, maintenance, server fees and more.

* : for the example above, we multiply by 1.5 to simplify our scenario. However, in the smart contract and because of the fact that the seller actually get 92% of the transaction, all of the coefficients are higher : in this case the value of your YTIcon would be worth around 0.6 ETH because we want our users to actually gain up to 1.5x more than what they invested.

Charity

With all of the cryptocurrency’s projects’ stories, we totally why you might be skeptical about us giving to charities and this is why our Smart Contract is publicly open for you to read and analyse it here.

In contrary of other similar cryptocurrencies projects, the donations are directly stored — through our Smart Contract — to an address with this specific purpose.

Finally, since a lot of the existing charities and organisations serve different purposes to make the world a better place, we will notify you through our Twitter (@YT_Icons) every time a change has been decided or when a donation has been done through this address so you can track it off thanks to the Blockchain.

We want our community to be aware of everything related to the charities and this is why we engage ourselves to provide you updates following every transaction done through this wallet and evidences once the Ether has been donated.
